Possible Solutions

To address the issue of insufficient sustainable and inclusive transport in India, a multi-faceted approach is necessary. Firstly, the government can invest in developing and promoting public transportation systems, such as electric buses, metros, and local trains, to reduce dependency on private vehicles. Incentivizing the use of electric and hybrid vehicles through subsidies and tax benefits can also encourage environmentally friendly transportation choices. Secondly, enhancing infrastructure for non-motorized transport, like cycling and walking, can be achieved by creating dedicated lanes and pathways in urban areas. This will promote a healthier lifestyle and contribute to reduced traffic congestion. Thirdly, integrating technology into the transportation system can help improve efficiency and accessibility. This includes implementing smart traffic management systems, real-time tracking of public transport, and developing mobile applications for seamless navigation and booking. Fourthly, promoting carpooling and ride-sharing initiatives can reduce the number of individual vehicles on the road, thus decreasing pollution and traffic. Collaborating with ride-hailing companies to introduce eco-friendly vehicles in their fleets can further support this goal. Lastly, educating the public about the benefits of sustainable and inclusive transport is crucial. Awareness campaigns and workshops can help change people's mindsets and encourage them to adopt eco-friendly practices in their daily commute.

An urban transport system considers all elements of transportation, including the infrastructure, rolling stock, and traffic flows, in a city as a functioning entity. The interrelationships among different system components are emphasized which is why this form of transport is the purest, where we can consider all the side effects and develop safe transport for the environment and inclusive for all people of all different conditions, which is why I along with Parisar suggest the shift to Sustainable Urban Transport Such as Electric Vehicles like cars, trams and buses which can efficiently transport people without consuming a lot of energy.
